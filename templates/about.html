About Company Knowledge Assistant

The Company Knowledge Assistant is a modern web application designed to help organizations efficiently search, manage, and access company policies, meeting notes, and other important documents using cutting-edge semantic search technology.

Powered by Cohere AI and MindsDB, this tool goes beyond simple keyword matching to understand the meaning of your queries, delivering more accurate and relevant results.

  • Natural Language Search: Find information using everyday languageâ€”no need for exact keywords.
  • Department Filtering: Narrow results by department such as HR, Marketing, Finance, or IT.
  • Browse All Documents: View the entire knowledge base at a glance.
  • Add New Documents: Easily upload or enter new policies, meetings, or other documents.

Why use Company Knowledge Assistant?

This tool is ideal for teams looking to streamline information retrieval, reduce time spent searching for documents, and ensure everyone has access to the latest company knowledge.
