About Company FAQ & Policy Assistant

AI-Powered
The Company FAQ & Policy Assistant helps employees quickly find answers, access policies, and get AI-powered summaries—all in one place.

Powered by OpenAI and MindsDB
  • Natural Language Search: Find answers using everyday language—no need for exact keywords.
  • Department Filtering: Browse by department (HR, Marketing, Finance, IT, Legal, etc.).
  • AI-Powered Summarization: Get concise summaries of any FAQ or policy.
  • Chat with AI: Ask questions in natural language and get instant answers.
  • Add New Content: Easily add new FAQs or policies.
Why use this assistant?
Streamline information retrieval, reduce time spent searching, and ensure everyone has access to the latest company knowledge.
